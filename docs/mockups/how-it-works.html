The journey

From sign-up to your first
paid consultation

No technical skills needed. Here's exactly how astrologers go live on Jyoti — usually in well under an hour.

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds with Google, or with your email and a one-time code. No passwords to remember, no credit card to start.

  • Continue with Google, or email + OTP
  • Pick your unique link, like jyoti.app/ravi-sharma
  • Set your timezone — we handle the rest
2

Make the page yours

Tell seekers who you are, and make the page feel like your practice — not like a generic tool.

  • Add your photo, story, experience, and social links
  • Upload your logo and pick your theme colour
  • Choose Hindi, English, or Hinglish — and an Indic font
3

Set your offerings & hours

Create consultation packages with your own prices, and decide when you're available. Jyoti makes sure two seekers can never grab the same slot.

  • Packages with durations, prices, discounts & coupons
  • Weekly hours, buffers, notice periods, daily limits
  • Ask seekers required questions before they book
4

Choose how you get paid

Your money, your account. Jyoti never holds a single rupee — payments flow straight to you.

  • Upload a UPI QR — seekers pay and add proof
  • Or connect your own gateway for instant confirmation
  • Receipts issued under your name & GST
5

Share & start consulting

Drop your link wherever your seekers already find you. Bookings, reminders, and receipts run themselves.

  • Share on Instagram, YouTube, or WhatsApp
  • Seekers book, pay, and get a Google Meet link
  • Add a team later — calls auto-share across them
