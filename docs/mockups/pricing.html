Pricing questions

Do you take a cut of my consultation fees? +

Never. Jyoti charges only a flat subscription. Every rupee a seeker pays goes directly to your own account — we never touch your money.

How do team seats work? +

Professional includes 1 seat and Studio includes 3. Need more helpers? Add extra seats at a flat per-seat monthly rate. Each seat is one consulting or accounts member.

Can I start for free? +

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever and lets you publish a branded page with UPI QR payments. Upgrade only when you're ready for your own gateway or a team.

What payment methods can my seekers use? +

On any plan, UPI QR with payment proof. On Professional and above, connect your own gateway (like Razorpay) for instant confirmation — funds settle straight to your bank.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Absolutely. There are no lock-ins. Cancel whenever you like and your page simply returns to the free tier limits.
