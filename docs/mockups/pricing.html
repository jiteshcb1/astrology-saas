Pricing questions

Do you take a cut of my consultation fees? +

Never. We charge only a flat subscription. Every rupee a seeker pays goes directly to your own UPI ID or Razorpay account — we never touch your money. That's the whole point.

How is this different from Topmate or Astrotalk? +

Topmate takes 10-13% of every booking. Astrotalk takes 20-30% from marketplace bookings. We charge a flat subscription regardless of how much you earn. The more you earn, the more you save with us.

How do team seats work? +

Pro includes 1 extra seat, Max includes 3. Each seat is one team member — either a Consulting member (takes sessions) or an Accounts member (sees revenue only). Add more seats anytime at ₹299/seat (Pro) or ₹499/seat (Max) per month.

Can I start completely free? +

Yes. Starter is free forever — no credit card, no trial period, no expiry. You get a real branded booking page with UPI payments and up to 30 bookings/month. Upgrade when you're ready.

What payment methods can seekers use? +

On all plans: UPI QR with payment proof (the consultant verifies manually). On Pro and above: connect your own Razorpay account for instant payment confirmation — funds settle directly to your bank within 2 business days.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Absolutely. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard anytime — your page returns to the free Starter limits.

Is there a setup fee or onboarding cost? +

No. You can set up your page, create packages, and go live in under 30 minutes — completely on your own. Enterprise customers get dedicated onboarding assistance included.

Do you offer a yearly discount? +

Yes. Pay yearly and save 17% — that's roughly 2 months free. Pro yearly: ₹7,999 (vs ₹9,588 monthly). Max yearly: ₹19,999 (vs ₹23,988 monthly).
